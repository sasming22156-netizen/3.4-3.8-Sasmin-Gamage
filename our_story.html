Our Story

Initially, Kiwi Cakes planned to develop a product featuring refillable air fresheners. However, after talking with Reegan (YES Spokesperson) about our air freshener idea, he encouraged the business to move away from it, as the market is quite saturated and the best way to sell it would be to businesses or large events (i.e., Fonterra). This is as if it isn’t sold in bulk, then it isn’t likely to succeed.

Our solution was to create a protein-rich cake that is affordable, convenient, and made using simple ingredients. By focusing on accessibility and value, Kiwi Cakes provides a realistic alternative to expensive protein powders and bars.

Kiwi Cakes was created to solve a common problem faced by many gym-goers: high-protein foods and supplements are often expensive, overly processed, or filled with artificial ingredients. Many students and low to middle-income individuals struggle to afford quality protein options that support their fitness goals.

Our target audience includes health-focused individuals aged 13–65, particularly gym-goers and eco-conscious consumers who want a product that aligns with both their fitness and sustainability values.
