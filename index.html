Executive Statement:

Kiwi Cakes is a business established in 2026 at Otago Boys’ High School under the Young Enterprise Scheme (YES). Kiwi Cakes has a high-protein snack designed for everyday gymgoers who want real results without spending a fortune on expensive supplements. We believe that good nutrition should be affordable, simple, and accessible to everyone. Kiwi Cakes combines quality ingredients with a strong New Zealand identity to create a product that fuels your goals naturally.

Our mission is simple: provide a powerful, affordable protein option that supports strength, recovery, and performance. Whether you're training after school, working out after work, or just trying to eat better, Kiwi Cakes is made to support your lifestyle. Ready to fuel your gains?
