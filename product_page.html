Product Information:

The pancake mix will come in a bag with 4-5 servings (5 pancakes per serving), including a serving scoop to accurately measure out a single serving. Instructions will be as simple as possible (“Take 1 scoop into a bowl, add 300ml of water/milk to the bowl, whisk/mix till smooth in consistency, etc., blah blah blah”). Each serving contains approximately 20g of protein, balanced carbohydrates for energy, and minimal added sugar.

Our Ingredients (per serving):

  • ½ cup Oat Flour
  • 2 tbsp Whey Protein Isolate
  • 1 tbsp Egg White Powder
  • 1 tbsp Buttermilk Powder
  • 1 tbsp Coconut Sugar
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • Pinch Sea Salt
  • ¼ tsp Cinnamon

We prioritise transparency in our nutritional information to help customers make informed decisions. Clear labelling and allergy information are provided to ensure consumer safety.
