Product Information:

Our Kiwi Cakes pancake mix is designed specifically for active individuals who want a quick, high-protein meal without sacrificing taste. Each pack contains 4–5 servings, with approximately 5 pancakes per serving. A scoop is included to ensure accurate portioning every time.

Preparation is simple and efficient: just add one scoop to a bowl, mix with water or milk, whisk until smooth, and cook. This makes Kiwi Cakes perfect for busy mornings, post-workout meals, or convenient snacks throughout the day.

Our Ingredients (per serving):

  • Oat Flour
  • Whey Protein Isolate
  • Egg White Powder
  • Buttermilk Powder
  • Coconut Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Pinch Sea Salt
  • Cinnamon

We prioritise transparency in our nutritional information to help customers make informed decisions. Clear labelling and allergy information are provided to ensure consumer safety.
